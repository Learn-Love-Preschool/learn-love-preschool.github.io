Local Program

Our local preschool is a full day program Monday-Friday from 8:00am-4:00pm, running September-June. We have a high-quality preschool program that is hands on and play based, following a creative curriculum, with a focus on social emotional development. We offer daily free choice and outside play, two healthy snacks in the AM and PM (child provides own lunch) and much more.
