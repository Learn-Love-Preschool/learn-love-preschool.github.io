Application Process

Learn and Love Preschool is now enrolling for September 2022! Please begin the application process below.

Step 1 - Contact Us

Please send an email here

Step 2 - Attend a Tour

We will arrange a time with the parent only for a personal tour of the school.

Step 3 - Application Form

Please fill out the application form and bring it to your tour or visit.

Step 4 - Observation Visit

Your child will have an individual 20 minute observation visit with the teacher.

Step 5 - Acceptance

If you would like to move forward and your child is a good fit for our program, we will offer your child a spot.
