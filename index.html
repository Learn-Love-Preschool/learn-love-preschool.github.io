Our Mission

Learn and Love Preschool provides a safe, developmentally appropriate, nurturing environment that encompasses the whole child. The curriculum is geared to support children’s social-emotional and cognitive development and academic and physical growth. It is created to entice the curious mind, promote a positive self-image, and form strong relationships. We are a play-based program because children learn best through hands on learning and engagement with the materials, environment, and people around them.


Our Philosophy

Learn and Love Preschool is based on the best practices of Creative, Montessori, and Play Curriculums. This will encourage children to have a blend of purposeful play and child directed activities. Our goal is for children to learn independence, become critical thinkers and problem solvers, form positive relationships, and learn academic skills in order to be successful in kindergarten.

In our program children’s natural creativity, wondrous curiosity, and individual spirit is encouraged, praised, and celebrated throughout the day. We see every child as unique and special in their own way. We celebrate diversity, accept all abilities, and believe in every form of love. Their voices are always heard and their character always seen. Learn and Love Preschool is a place of acceptance and a lifetime of learning, where we learn to love everyone and love to learn everything.
